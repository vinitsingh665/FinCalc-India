Understanding SIP

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest small amounts periodically in mutual funds. It inculcates the habit of saving and benefits from the power of compounding and rupee cost averaging.

SIP Formula

FV = P × ({[1 + i]^n - 1} / i) × (1 + i)
