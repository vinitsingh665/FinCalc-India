Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

Welcome to FinCalc India. We prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. No Data Collection

FinCalc India operates as a client-side utility. All calculations (SIP, Tax, EMI, etc.) are performed directly in your web browser using JavaScript. We do not transmit, store, or save your financial inputs on any server. When you close or refresh the page, your data is cleared.

2. Cookies and Analytics

We use standard, anonymous analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand website traffic sources and popular pages. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

3. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fincalcindia.com.
